Introduction

In this milestone, we are working on designing a TOPY (Together, Old, Pet and Young) system. TOPY helps match senior people with pets and young kids and make sure their experience in TOPY station is fun and safe. To achieve that, in our system, we have a criminal check process and dedicated registration process. And this registration process will be our super component and focus of our project.

System Description

As shown in Figure 1, in general, there are 3 main steps for people who come into the TOPY system to go through. First is a criminal check, it will check the identity of the old, pet owner or parents to make sure people staying in the station will be valid and safe. Then, there is a super component of registration for matching pets, children and old together. And at last is a claiming process that includes old leaving, pets claimed by the owner and children claimed by parents.

And figure 2 that is given in the next page uses a Petri net to show what it is like inside the super component. We design three scenarios that handle different identities of people’s registration: Old, Young and Pet.

Figure 2 Expanded C-card Petri net for super component

1. Old

When the old come to TOPY station, TOPY station will divide its working time into half hour time slots. Old will pick time slots and tell allergy information (or called it time duration) they will stay and stations will store the picked time slots into a database along with old’s name , date of birth and allergy information. And it will have 2 additional fields in the table of the old, pet and young, to indicate the young or pet that is assigned to this old. If an old person is allergic to all kinds of pets, the pet field would always be filled.

2. Young

Parents may take kids to TOPY station. When they do so, TOPY station will provide all the old with free young slots along with their available time slots. Then, parents need to pick one old person and their time slot. TOPY station will then assign this kid to the old that parent picked and store the registration information into the database.

3. People with pet

Pet owners can take his/her pet into the TOPY station. Similar to the young scenario, the TOPY station provides old with free pet slots along with their available time slots. Owner picks an old time slot. Station stores information into the database.

Figure 3 The class diagram of our super component

The senior citizens can sign up for some available time slots,when they can take care of the children or the pets and also they can update the time slots.The parent can sign up their pet and children, and the matching class will return a bunch of recommendation candidates for the parent to choose. And the parent can pick up its children or pets.  

The matching class is our core class in this system. The system will fetch the child or pet table  and senior table to do the matching process when parents sign up their children or pets or both or senior citizens come inside the TOPY station.The event tracker will track all currently in processing events, and provide some basic information.

Implementation

Figure 4 : Deployment diagram of Our TOPY system

Figure 4 shows the deployment of our system. We plan to deploy the project on AWS. The web application is implemented using Javascript with library support such as React.JS and Node.JS.

The front end will provide senior citizens with several functionalities, such as registration, finding a match, or signing up for a free time slot. And it also provides parents with several functionalities just like we talk about in super components such as pick up.

We will use one database system consisting of Parent table, Pet table,Child table,Senior table ,event table and we choose to use dynamo DB in AWS.And we will write Graph QL to communicate the front end system with the backend of the system - the dynamo Database and perform CRUD operations.
